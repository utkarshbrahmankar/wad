About Us

About PICT

Our History

Established in 1983, Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is one of the premier engineering colleges in Pune, affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, producing globally competent and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

To provide quality technical education in line with global trends and the needs of industry.

Departments

Academic Departments

Computer Engineering

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in 1983. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

Head of Department: Mrs. Geetanjali Kale

Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology focuses on developing professionals in the fields of software development, networking, and database management.

Head of Department: Mrs. Archana Ghotkar

Electronics & Telecommunication

The Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering offers comprehensive education in electronic systems, communication technologies, and signal processing.

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

The newest department at PICT, focuses on cutting-edge technologies in AI, machine learning, and data analytics.
