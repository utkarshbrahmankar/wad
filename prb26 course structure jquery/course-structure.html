PICT - Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) Course Structure

First Year (FE)

  • Mathematics I

    Introduction to calculus, differential equations, and their applications in engineering.

  • Physics I

    Fundamentals of mechanics, optics, and thermodynamics.

  • C Programming

    Basics of programming in C, data types, loops, functions, and arrays.

Second Year (SE)

  • Mathematics II

    Advanced calculus, Fourier series, and partial differential equations.

  • Digital Electronics

    Study of logic gates, combinational circuits, and flip-flops.

  • Data Structures

    Introduction to linear data structures like arrays, stacks, and queues.

Third Year (TE)

  • Mathematics III

    Laplace transforms, complex analysis, and statistical methods in engineering.

  • Computer Networks

    Study of network protocols, OSI model, and practical networking concepts.

  • Operating Systems

    Understanding of process management, memory management, and file systems in modern OS.

Final Year (BE)

  • Software Engineering

    Principles of software development, design, testing, and maintenance.

  • Project Management

    Planning, scheduling, and managing resources for successful project execution.

  • Machine Learning

    Introduction to machine learning algorithms and applications in real-world problems.
